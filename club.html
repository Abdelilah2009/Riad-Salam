Basket Club

Promoting physical fitness and teamwork, the Basket Club inspires students to develop their skills in basketball while building confidence and coordination. Through training and friendly matches, students foster collaboration, strategic thinking, and a sense of achievement in a supportive environment. Sint!

Chess Club

Encouraging critical thinking and strategic planning, the Chess Club challenges students to expand their problem-solving skills. It’s a space where minds meet, strategies evolve, and friendships are forged—all while nurturing creativity and focus through the timeless game of chess. Sint!

Ping Pong Club

The Ping Pong Club provides students with an exciting way to improve focus, hand-eye coordination, and agility. Beyond the fast-paced matches, it’s a place where students can build friendships, develop sportsmanship, and foster a love for healthy competition in a supportive and fun environment

Football Club

The Football Club is all about teamwork, perseverance, and sportsmanship. Students not only enhance their physical fitness and technical skills but also learn the importance of collaboration and leadership. Whether on the field or off, the club fosters a sense of community and a passion for the beautiful game.

Drawing Club

Unleashing creativity and self-expression, the Drawing Club provides students with a canvas to explore their artistic potential. Through colors, sketches, and imagination, members develop their technical skills while embracing the joy of art and the freedom to convey their unique perspectives. Sint!

Theater Club

Bringing stories to life, the Theater Club empowers students to showcase their talents in acting, public speaking, and teamwork. Whether performing on stage or behind the scenes, members learn confidence, collaboration, and the magic of storytelling through dynamic performances. Sint!
