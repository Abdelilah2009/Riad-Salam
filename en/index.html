Welcome to RIAD SALAM School

Riad Salam is a school in MARRAKECH, Dar Salam

At Riad Salam School, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning. Located in Marrakech, Dar Salam, our school provides a supportive environment where students thrive academically, socially, and creatively. Join us on a journey of discovery and excellence.

About Us

Our Key Features:

Excellency in Education:

We provide a well-rounded curriculum that focuses on academic excellence and critical thinking.

Innovative Learning:

Embracing technology and modern teaching methods to prepare students for the future.

Personal Growth:

We encourage character development, teamwork, and leadership.

Cultural Values:

Celebrating diversity and fostering respect for all cultures.

Our Clubs

Football Club

This club focuses on building teamwork and physical fitness through regular football training and friendly matches ⚽.

Basket Club

This club encourages students to develop their basketball skills and teamwork by organizing practice sessions and tournaments 🏀.

Ping Pong Club

This club offers a space for students to enhance their ping pong techniques while enjoying friendly competitions 🏓.

Chess

The Chess Club boosts strategic thinking, improves focus, and develops problem-solving skills ♟.

Drawing

The Drawing Club inspires creativity, enhances artistic skills, and encourages self-expression 🎨.

Theater

The Drama Club helps students build confidence, improve communication, and explore their creativity through teamwork and self-expression 🎭.
